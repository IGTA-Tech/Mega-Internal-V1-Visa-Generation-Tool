# Document 7: Filing Completeness Checklist

## Document Purpose

A pure checklist of what is required to file a complete petition. This document does NOT assess quality or provide recommendations - it simply tracks whether each required component is present and complete.

**Focus:** File completeness only. Is everything needed present? Yes or No.

## Critical Reminders

- **O-1A/O-1B/P-1A**: Advisory opinion is REQUIRED
- **O-1A/O-1B/P-1A**: Itinerary is REQUIRED
- **EB-1A**: NO advisory opinion required, NO itinerary required
- **EB-1A**: Kazarian two-step analysis REQUIRED in Legal Brief
- **P-1A**: NO comparable evidence (unlike O-1A, O-1B, EB-1A)
- **All Types**: Verify current USCIS filing fees before submitting

---

## Prohibited Content

This document should NEVER include:
1. Quality assessments (how strong the case is)
2. Approval probability predictions
3. Timeline estimates
4. Recommendations or advice
5. RFE risk analysis

This is ONLY a completeness checklist. Quality assessment belongs in Documents 4 and 9.
